<p>This repository is governed by following code of conduct guidelines.</p>
<p>We put collaboration, trust, respect and transparency as core values for our community.<br>
Our community welcomes participants from all over the world with different experience,<br>

<p>We have adopted this code of conduct and require all contributors to agree with that to build a healthy,<br>

<p>The guideline is aimed to support a community where all people should feel safe to participate,<br>
introduce new ideas and inspire others, regardless of:</p>
<ul>
<li>Age</li>
<li>Gender</li>
<li>Gender identity or expression</li>
<li>Family status</li>
<li>Marital status</li>
<li>Ability</li>
<li>Ethnicity</li>
<li>Race</li>
<li>Sex characteristics</li>
<li>Sexual identity and orientation</li>
<li>Education</li>
<li>Native language</li>
<li>Background</li>
<li>Caste</li>
<li>Religion</li>
<li>Geographic location</li>
<li>Socioeconomic status</li>
<li>Personal appearance</li>
<li>Any other dimension of diversity</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="toc-header" id="our-standards"><a href="#our-standards" class="toc-anchor">¶</a> Our Standards</h2>
<p>We are welcoming the following behavior:</p>
<ul>
<li>Be respectful for different ideas, opinions and points of view</li>
<li>Be constructive and professional</li>
<li>Use inclusive language</li>
<li>Be collaborative and show the empathy</li>
<li>Focus on the best results for the community</li>
</ul>
<p>The following behavior is unacceptable:</p>
<ul>
<li>Violence, threats of violence, or inciting others to commit self-harm</li>
<li>Personal attacks, trolling, intentionally spreading misinformation, insulting/derogatory comments</li>
<li>Public or private harassment</li>
<li>Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission</li>
<li>Derogatory language</li>
<li>Encouraging unacceptable behavior</li>
<li>Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community</li>
</ul>
<h2 class="toc-header" id="our-responsibilities"><a href="#our-responsibilities" class="toc-anchor">¶</a> Our Responsibilities</h2>
<p>Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of the Code of Conduct<br>
and are expected to take appropriate actions in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.</p>
<p>Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments,<br>
commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned<br>
to this Code of Conduct, or to ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for other behaviors<br>
that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.</p>
<h2 class="toc-header" id="reporting"><a href="#reporting" class="toc-anchor">¶</a> Reporting</h2>
<p>If you believe you’re experiencing unacceptable behavior that will not be tolerated as outlined above,<br>
please report to <code>opensourcegroup@netcracker.com</code>. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response<br>
that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality<br>
with regard to the reporter of an incident.</p>
<p>Please also report if you observe a potentially dangerous situation, someone in distress, or violations of these guidelines,<br>
even if the situation is not happening to you.</p>
